About the Baschus Real Estate

The Baschus Real Estate is a cooperative information network of Real Estate Offices that provide descriptions of most of the homes and properties for sale in Bergen, Essex, Passaic, and Hudson Counties and beyond. It insures that your property's information will be available to be seen by all the other members in the association.

Selling or buying your home is not an easy task. A Baschus Real Estate member will take you through the entire process, oversee the details, and guide you every step of the way. By working with a member of the Baschus Real Estate you have a professional on your side that has access to the largest number of listings, or access to the largest number of available buyers, through the Baschus Real Estate. A Baschus Real Estate member is committed to serving you!
